About

Hello there! Welcome to the official website for Create Jam.

What is Create Jam?

We are a bi-annual game jam, taking place at the Create during spring and fall. Our mission is to foster the game development environment in Aalborg.

Why should I attend?

Maybe you wanna get into game development, or maybe you are already a game dev looking to take your skills further.

Create Jam is an opportunity to get to know new people, working on your skills or obtain new ones, get insight into the industry from talks, and overall just having fun.

When is the next jam?

Our next edition is in Fall. Sign up and we will shoot you an email when we have the date set. You can also like us on Facebook.
