Create Jam - Spring 2020

Noir Spring edition

Some wizard and the mystery of the smoking owl

This edition became the first Create Jam edition to be hosted entirely online. Submissions where voted according to the criteria of solid gameplay and creative take on the (pseudo-randomly generated) theme. A lot of the submissions also related to the noir aesthetic of this edition.

With a concept relating to the zeitgeist and a beautifully excecuted game, Team Escargots (AKA Set Snail ) took home the win with "The Social Distancing Owl".

You can check out the submissions here.

Create Jam - Fall 2019

Level up spring edition

Level up a mechanic

Create Jam levelled up by partnering up with Game Hub and Business Aalborg. As a result, we had 148 attendant and 36 games created, both being recordbreakers for Create Jam.

Team Cherio made a game best described as slime volleyball on steriods. Their game Capitalistic and Haughty Extraordinaires Engaging in Riveting and Intriguing Occupation (C.H.E.E.R.I.O.) employed crazy powerups to embody the theme and ultimately took home the community award.

(kill Pell' (industry) Hangover Santa (boardgame))

Create Jam - Spring 2019

Retro spring edition

Desperately seeking high-speed skater

With a synthwave 80s aesthetic and our pseudo-random theme selector being on point, the backdrop for the jam was set. 15 itch.io submissions. 5 boardgames. 1 awesome weekend.

Retro Skullzzz, AKA Octohat Games won their 3rd Create Jam, this time with their game "Maya Mash". Apart from the community award, we had prizes for best boardgame as well as the industry award - the game with the most release potential.

You can find Maya Mash here and all other submissions here.
